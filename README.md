## How to Use

1. Enter a stock ticker symbol (e.g., AAPL, JNJ, KO)
2. Click "Search" to fetch current dividend data
3. Enter the number of shares you own (or plan to own)
4. View your projected dividend income instantly

The calculator automatically shows:
- **Annual Dividend Income** - Total dividends per year
- **Per Payment** - Amount per dividend payment (based on payment frequency)
- **Monthly** - Estimated monthly dividend income
